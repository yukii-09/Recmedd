About us

Established in 2024, RECMed Enterprise OPC is a pharmaceutical company based in Bacoor, Cavite, that delves into the field of wholesale drug distributors. The products offered are purely ethical in promoting high-quality medicines and adhering to worldwide quality standards. The various products include antibiotics, proton pump inhibitors, beta-lactamase inhibitors, and vitamins. Moreover, it is committed to supptying cost effective and safe drugs and medicine to most hospitals and healtheare institutions. This company aims to delivering economicat and affordable pharmaceutical products that live through a great quality of drugs, which ensures easy access to health and wellness for the patients.
